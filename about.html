Welcome to FIXD — where we believe a clean home is a happy home. We are a leading professional cleaning company based in Dubai, dedicated to providing exceptional home and office cleaning services across the UAE.

Founded on the principles of reliability, professionalism, and genuine care for our clients, FIXD has grown to become one of Dubai's most trusted maid services, with hundreds of 5-star reviews from satisfied homeowners across the city.

Every member of our team is personally vetted, professionally trained, and covered by our comprehensive insurance — so you can relax knowing your home is in safe hands.
